# Internal AI Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines how enterprise AI systems, including AI agents and LLM-powered workflows, should be used inside the organization.

## Approved AI Usage

AI systems may be used for:

- Internal knowledge search
- Draft generation
- Support triage
- Runbook lookup
- Compliance evidence review
- Summarization of approved documents

## Restricted AI Usage

AI systems must not be used to:

- Make final employment decisions
- Approve financial transactions without human review
- Access confidential customer records without authorization
- Override security controls
- Execute production changes without approval
- Store sensitive data in unauthorized systems

## Agent Requirements

Enterprise AI agents must:

- Use approved tools only
- Follow least privilege
- Produce audit evidence
- Respect user authorization
- Use source grounding for policy answers
- Require human approval for high-risk actions

## Policy Decision

If an AI agent needs to access confidential data or perform a system-changing action, the request must be routed to policy review before execution.
